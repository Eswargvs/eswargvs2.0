About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with over 6 years of experience in building scalable and efficient web applications. My journey began at Vellore Institute of Technology, where I earned my Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication Engineering, developing a strong foundation in both hardware and software domains.

Throughout my career, I've worked with leading organizations including TCS, Emproto Technologies, and currently at Amadeus Software Labs, where I've honed my skills in full-stack development, DevOps, and cloud-native technologies. My expertise spans across modern web development frameworks, cloud platforms, and container orchestration tools.

I specialize in creating robust, scalable, and maintainable applications using technologies like React, Node.js, Angular, and Python. My recent focus has been on cloud-native development with Kubernetes, OpenShift, and containerization tools like Docker and Podman. I'm passionate about implementing CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure as code practices to streamline development processes.

As a problem solver, I enjoy tackling complex technical challenges and delivering high-quality solutions that meet business requirements. I'm committed to staying updated with the latest industry trends and continuously improving my skills through hands-on projects and certifications.
